Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Heated Vest work?

Our Heated Vest uses advanced carbon fiber heating technology with 3 heating zones (chest, back, and core) that warm up instantly when connected to a USB power bank. Simply plug in your power bank and adjust the temperature settings to your preference.

How long does the battery last?

Battery life depends on the power bank capacity and temperature setting. On low heat, a 10,000mAh power bank typically lasts 6-8 hours. On high heat, expect 3-4 hours. The vest works with any standard USB power bank.

Is it safe to wear?

Yes! Our heated vest includes built-in overheat protection and automatic shut-off features. The heating elements are safely integrated into the fabric, and the vest is designed to be worn comfortably all day.

Can I wash the vest?

Yes, the vest is machine washable. Simply remove the USB connector and power bank before washing. Follow the care instructions on the label for best results.

What is the warranty?

We offer a comprehensive 90-day money-back guarantee on all our heated vests. Contact our support team for warranty details and any questions.
